Original Music List

The original Doom 64 soundtrack was composed by Aubrey Hodges. In the Nintendo 64 code each music track is referenced to by number, where the music tracks begin after the sound designations (which end at 92). On the PC engines the music tracks are given specific names to reference to. In addition to the prior information, the table below lists the levels each track appears in and the title given to the track by Aubrey Hodges.
