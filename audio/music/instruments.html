Instrument List

The music of Doom 64 is fundamentally MIDI with a custom soundset based on the sounds listed in the audio assets. Many PC ports store the custom soundset as a soundfont file which organizes the sounds as instruments. Below is how the instruments are ordered with their General MIDI designations and descriptions.
