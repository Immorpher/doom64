Audio Assets

Doom 64 used 124 unique sounds, the first 33 of which were for the soundtrack. These sounds assets mainly originated from royalty-free audio archives, which were also used by films and other games. The remaining sounds were made by Aubrey Hodges, sampled from synthesizers, or sampled from the PS1 Doom soundtrack. Not all of their origins have been found, however the table below was created from the work by the PSX/Doom 64 High-Def SFX Project.
