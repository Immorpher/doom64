Thing Flags

Here are properties assignable to any thing in Doom 64. However not all properties will have an effect on all things. Introduced by Doom 64 EX, the "Nightmare Creature" flag gives enemies a combat boost and green color. For other things, they will only inherit the green color. This flag is supported on all PC engines, and some recent Nintendo 64 engines as well.

Flag BitEditor SettingDescription
0x1Easy DifficultyThing is on skill levels 1 & 2 (Be Gentle and Bring It On)
0x2Medium DifficultyThing is on skill level 3 (I Own Doom)
0x4Hard DifficultyThing is on skill levels 4 & 5 (Watch Me Die and harder)
0x8Ambush PlayersDo not react to player attack sound (deaf)
0x10Multiplayer Only(unused) Thing is not in single player
0x20Spawned by SpecialDon't spawn until triggered with action or macro
0x40Trigger On Pick UpTrigger tagged linedef with matching TID when picked up
0x80Trigger On DeathTrigger tagged linedef with matching TID when killed
0x100Count Secret On Pick UpCount as secret for intermission when picked up
0x200No InfightingNever fight other monsters
0x400No Spawn in Deathmatch(unused) Do not spawn thing in deathmatch mode
0x800No Spawn in Multiplayer(unused) Do not spawn thing in any multiplayer mode
0x1000Nightmare Creature(new) Give monster double health, fast speed, and green color
